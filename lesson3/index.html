Site name:I weather

Description

I Weather offers unique weather forecasts in over 100 languages and dialects with Superior Accuracy™ for all corners of the world – more local than any other government or private weather forecast service.

Site propose:

Provide accurate and up-to-date information to rural producers and fishermen on weather forecasts in their locality and differentiated by production. Through a portal and an application (Android and IOS), it will be possible to access a navigable rain, temperature and humidity forecast map for the next seven days.
